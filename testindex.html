    • But this website only focuses on Freestyle & BreastStroke.


    In swimming there are some rule you have that you have to follow here is a list of some of the important ones

    • Touching the ground in any way
    • Stopping on the ground or on a lane line
    • Going before the whistle
    • Touching the water before your teammate touches the wall in a relay
    • Getting the pool when it is not your turn to swim
